HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Rangefinder

An ultrasonic rangefinder determines the distance to objects in the same way as dolphins or bats do. It generates sound pulses at a frequency of 40 kHz and listens to the echo. By the time of propagation of the sound wave back and forth, it is possible to uniquely determine the distance to the object.
